Unique Stationery Stores

A little about of each store

Yoseka Stationery
Located in Greenpoint since 2020, you'll find the same set up of a tester table located centrally in their shop. Over the years, the team has grown to a lot more people than just the store's owners, but the heart of Yoseka and their mission will always be to create a space where anyone can try out stationery within a community of individuals who share the same passion.
Baum-kuchen
Their journey started in 2010 as an online store operated in Wakako and Frido's garage. Today they are a studio, shop (online and brick & mortar) and lifestyle who are passionate about living life and excited to share with anyone everything extraordinary that inspires every day. They are interested in exploring the idea of "Journey as the destination", "love for analogue" and "slow living" and are constantly evolving and looking for inspiration from Japan (where Wakako grew up), Germany (where Frido is from) and everywhere in between (especially in Los Angeles where their home and studio is). They opened their brick and mortar studio/shop in 2013 where they showcase some of their products and share their stories. Their current studio/shop is in the heart of Altadena.
Omoi
OMOI is a life goods select shop operating in downtown Philadelphia since 2006. The store offers a selection of items that the owners like, use, and believe in, with an emphasis on thoughtful design, purpose, and a sense of humor. If you like stationery, staff-selected books, cool accessories, and useful things for your daily life, they invite you to stop by.
Niconeco Zakkaya
Their beginnings trace back to their Etsy shop, established in December 2015. The name "zakkaya" is derived from the Japanese word "zakka," encompassing all things that enhance your home, lifestyle, and appearance. These small, yet meaningful details can truly elevate your life, create memories, and even spark inspiration. The shop offers a thoughtfully curated selection of exquisite, hand-crafted items from Japan to the rest of the world. Their primary objective is to offer exquisite tools that invigorate imaginative minds.
